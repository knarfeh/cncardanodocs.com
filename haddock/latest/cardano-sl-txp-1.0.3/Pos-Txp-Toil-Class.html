Description

Type classes for Toil abstraction. * MonadUtxoRead and MonadUtxo for encapsulation of Utxo storage. * MonadStakesRead and MonadStakes for encapsulation of Stakes storage. * MonadTxPoll for encapsulation of mem pool of local transactions.
